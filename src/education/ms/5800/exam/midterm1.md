# Midterm 1

Hi everyone,

We’ve received several questions regarding the cheat sheet policy for the upcoming midterm. To ensure fairness and consistency for all students, we take following policy as previous year did:

- **No Personal Cheat Sheets:** Students are not permitted to bring their own handwritten or printed cheat sheets into the exam.
- **Provided Reference Sheet:** A standardized reference sheet will be provided to you. It will be attached as the first page of your exam paper.
- **Content:** This sheet will contain all the essential equations, constants, and definitions required to complete the exam.

However, we still encourage you to manually write a *sheet* so you can summarize what you learned and improve your overall understanding, but please do not *cheat* in the exam. Welcome to our OH if you have any questions, good luck and we will release the official reference sheet later. 

Best of luck with your revision, 

**CIS580 Team**
